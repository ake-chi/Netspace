About Us

A brand-new way to discover, educate and entertain.

NetSpace is your go-to VR arcade for the best virtual reality experience in Sydney. With our growing selection of solo and multiplayer games, players of all ages can seamlessly enjoy a variety of action, adventure and simulation-based experiences!

VR Cafe

Our team is happy to announce that we are finally launching our newest and most unique virtual reality experience to date –VR Café.

VR Café offers new and exciting ways to connect. It is an online virtual community space that lets you interact not just with people within the country, but to all NetSpace users and players all over the world.
